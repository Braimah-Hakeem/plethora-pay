About Us

Plethorapay Solutions Ltd is a financial technology company comprised of industry experts with the goal of simplifying payment/payment collection and ensuring accessibility for everyone. We believe the world is better with a diversified, inclusive, payment ecosystem. Which is why we aim to eradicate payment/payment collection hassles for Traders, Merchants, and Customers, via our innovative platforms and solutions that enable businesses to adapt their payments stack to support their business strategy, and expand to new markets fast, while keeping payments secure and in compliance.

  • Public Private Partnership Models
  • Foreign Direct Investment
  • Global Consultancy
